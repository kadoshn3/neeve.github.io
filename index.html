MY STORY

Matt, Est. 2017

Matt was created circa a snowboarding trip, Neeve was on route and stopped for breakfast at a bagel shop. Neeve was tired of people spelling and mispronouncing his name (Plus it'd be funny), decided it would be easier to spell Matt than his own. After waiting several minutes for his bagel, the same woman Neeve orders from, stares at Neeve with his bagel in hand: "Matt... Matt... MATT!" Until his friends screamed at Neeve, making him realize he was Matt. From that point, Neeve birthed Matt, a new beginning.

AN UNEXPECTED JOURNEY TO GOOGLE

Google December 1st, 2017

Matt started college with interest in working for Google to a professor that he had no idea would change his life. After months of planning, Neeve and his five best friends began their incredible journey to Google. This once in a lifetime opportunity was filled with obstacles and lasting memories. Simply entering their site was breathtaking, welcomed by the CEO to give our group a tour we would never forget. From nap pods to airplane themed to the floor of fun, we were ecstatic, we never wanted to leave. If the gourmet food wasn't enough to motivate any single one of us, the fact that its Google did.
