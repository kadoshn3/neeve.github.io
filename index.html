ORIGIN STORY

Matt, Est. 2016

Matt was created circa a snowboarding trip, Neeve was on route and stopped for breakfast at a bagel shop. Neeve was tired of people spelling and mispronouncing his name (Plus it'd be funny), decided it would be easier to spell Matt than his own. After waiting several minutes for his bagel, the same woman Neeve orders from, stares at Neeve with his bagel in hand: "Matt... Matt... MATT!" Until his friends screamed at Neeve, making him realize he was Matt. From that point, Neeve birthed Matt, a new beginning.

More on Matt ×

Once Matt was born, Neeve could not contain himself and would use Matt at every coffee shop, bagel location, and restaurant where an employee would ask of his name. The best is when people who know Neeve, and are in line with him to order don't know about Matt, and they get so perplexed when he tells them his name is Matt.
As a barista at Starbucks, Neeve would always have to ask for names and has experienced mispronouncing and mispelling names very often. His favorite experiences at Starbucks include meeting other Matt's and meeting other people who use fake names. There are these two people who come to Starbucks everyday after work at 5:45pm and use a name of who annoys them most at work which is absolutely incredible.

Max Speed 61 mph

AN UNEXPECTED JOURNEY TO GOOGLE

Google December 1st, 2017

Matt started college with interest in working for Google to a professor that he had no idea would change his life. After months of planning, Neeve and his five best friends began their incredible journey to Google. This once in a lifetime opportunity was filled with obstacles and lasting memories. Simply entering their site was breathtaking, welcomed by the CEO to give our group a tour we would never forget. From nap pods to airplane themed rooms to the floor of fun, we were ecstatic, we never wanted to leave. If the gourmet food wasn't enough to motivate any single one of us, the fact that its Google did.

Matt's Google Experience Inspires Him Everyday ×

Google motivates Matt, it drives his school work and keeps him on top of anything that could distract Matt from his goal.
