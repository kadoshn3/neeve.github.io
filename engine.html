Why did Matt choose Rowan University?

Engineering Building

In 1992, Henry M. Rowan donated to Glassboro State College $100 000 000. He had the two desires with that money, to create a new engineering building and increase the quality of Rowan's academics, and to change the name of the school after himself. Over 20 years later, in 2016 Rowan University had just finished building a brand new top class engineering building. Quickly jumping them in rank to become 19th top engineering school in the country. Matt came to Rowan in the fall 2017 because he would get to use all the brand new equipment the new tech rooms would have to offer. When he came to an open house, he was unsure about his major, but he learned that one senior team were sending a satellite into space and one year later, is now orbitting our Earth. With brand new chairs, collaboration rooms, Einstein Bagels, flat screen tv's, and a whole floor dedicated to the Electrical and Computer Engineering Department. Not to mention that Rowan has one of the largest student branches of IEEE in the world.

Right School 100%
